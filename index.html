    • Enrichment Activities

    In Year 1, the children enjoy a variety of enrichment activities. We have 3 exciting Entry Point Days where the children enjoy taking part in different activities and get to learn all about the stars in a space! They enjoy a Panto at Christmas time as well as a Christmas Church Visit and they love to visit our local Petts Wood Library and Crofton Woods. In Year 1, the children go on an exciting school trip and enjoy performing to their friends and family in their Easter show as well as their class assemblies.

    Term Themes

    Pirates

    During the Autumn Term, children begin learning what history is. They enjoy learning who Blackbeard is and begin understanding chronology by ordering events and peopple in order.

    space

    In the Spring Term, the children learn about astronauts, when we landed on the moon and how that changed the world.

    Keepers of the wild

    The children will learn about who David Attenborough is and the work he does. They also being learning to use sources to articulate ideas and thoughts.
